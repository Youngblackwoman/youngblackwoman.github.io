YAY!!! Thank God we made it to the end of March 2012. This is not really a post but I just want to appreciate everybody born in the month of March. It’s been a pretty good month for me because I had a lot of people celebrating (including me).  Each day presented somebody’s birthday which in my own language means cake (don’t hate me). Well after doing a lot of research (yeah right), I have concluded that about 30% of the people in the world were born in march. I thank God for giving us the grace to celebrate our birthday(s) this year…I pray 2013 would be better (can I get an AMEN somebody).
